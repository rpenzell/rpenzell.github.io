Trip Overview

In July and August of 2017, the extended Saunders family, which also includes the Yanow and Penzell families, embarked on the vacation of a lifetime. Over the course of 10 days, we explored the beautiful country of Tanzania on safari, as we experienced everything their culture had to offer. This goal of this page is to serve as a home for everyone's pictures and stories, so we can keep replaying the greatest trip ever.

Where we traveled

  • Ngorongoro
  • Serengeti
